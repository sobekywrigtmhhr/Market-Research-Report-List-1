<p><p>The RFID Electronic Toll Collection System is an advanced technology that utilizes Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) for automated toll collection on highways and expressways. The technology enhances efficiency for both toll operators and users, as transactions are processed seamlessly.</p><p>Market growth for the RFID Electronic Toll Collection System is being driven by increasing vehicular traffic, the need for efficient transportation infrastructure, and government initiatives to upgrade toll collection methods.
