<p><strong>The Antihypertensive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diuretics</li><li>Angiotensin Converting Enzyme (ACE) Inhibitors</li><li>Angiotensin Receptor Blockers (ARBs)</li><li>Calcium Channel Blockers</li><li>Beta Blockers</li><li>Alpha Blockers</li><li>Vasodilators & Renin Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antihypertensive market encompasses various drug types aimed at managing high blood pressure. Diuretics reduce fluid retention, while ACE inhibitors and ARBs block pathways that cause blood vessel constriction. Calcium channel blockers relax heart and vessel muscles, and beta blockers lower heart rate and workload. Alpha blockers help relax blood vessels, vasodilators directly widen them, and renin inhibitors reduce hormone activity that raises blood pressure. Each class targets different mechanisms to effectively control hypertension.</p></p>
